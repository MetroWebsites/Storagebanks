Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Storage Banks

How do I rent a storage unit?

You can rent a unit by calling us at (660) 886-7471 or visiting one of our locations. We'll help you choose the right size unit and complete the rental process quickly.

What size storage unit do I need?

Unit size depends on what you're storing. A 5x10 holds a bedroom's contents, 10x10 fits a small apartment, 10x20 accommodates a full house, and 10x30 can store vehicles or large amounts of items. Call us for personalized recommendations!

Can I access my unit anytime?

Most of our locations offer 24/7 access. Some locations have specific hours. Please call ahead or ask during rental to confirm access hours for your specific location.

How much does storage cost?

Prices vary by location and unit size, ranging from $35-$118/month for standard units. RV storage ranges from $45-$110/month depending on size. Visit our location pages for detailed pricing.

Is there a lease term or contract?

No! We offer flexible month-to-month rentals. You can rent for as long as you need without being locked into a long-term contract.

Do I need insurance?

Yes, insurance is required. You can purchase our affordable Tenant Protection Plan or provide proof of your own insurance policy that covers stored items.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, credit cards, and debit cards. You can also pay online through our secure payment portal for added convenience.

Can I cancel my rental?

Yes, you can cancel anytime with proper notice. However, there will be no refund of rent for any reason. If you change your mind, only the deposit is refunded.

Are your facilities secure?

Yes! All our facilities feature secure fencing, and you provide your own lock for your unit. We take security seriously to protect your belongings.

Can I store a vehicle?

Absolutely! We have outdoor parking spaces at our Lexington Ave location and a dedicated RV lot in Odessa. We can accommodate cars, trucks, RVs, boats, trailers, and more.

What items cannot be stored?

You cannot store hazardous materials, flammable liquids, explosives, perishable items, illegal items, or anything with strong odors. See our policies page for a complete list.

Do you have climate-controlled units?

Please contact us at (660) 886-7471 to inquire about climate-controlled options at specific locations. Availability varies by facility.
