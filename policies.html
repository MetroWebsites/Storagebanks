Rental Policies

Terms and Conditions for Storage Banks LLC

Payment & Rental Terms

  • Rental Period: Month-to-month rentals with no long-term contract required
  • Rent Due Date: Rent is due on the 1st of each month
  • Late Fees: A late fee will be applied after the 10th of the month
  • Payment Methods: Cash, check, credit card, debit card, or online payments accepted
  • Security Deposit: Required at move-in; refundable upon proper move-out
  • No Refunds: There will be no refund of rent for any reason. If you change your mind, only the deposit is refunded

Insurance Requirements

  • Required: All tenants must have insurance coverage for stored items
  • Options: Purchase our Tenant Protection Plan or provide proof of your own insurance
  • Coverage Amount: Must be adequate to cover the value of your stored items
  • Facility Not Liable: Storage Banks LLC is not responsible for damage, loss, or theft of stored items

Prohibited Items

The following items are NOT allowed to be stored in our facilities:

  • Hazardous materials or chemicals
  • Flammable liquids, gases, or explosives
  • Firearms, weapons, or ammunition
  • Illegal drugs or contraband
  • Perishable food items
  • Living plants or animals
  • Items with strong or offensive odors
  • Toxic or radioactive materials
  • Stolen property
  • Cash or negotiable instruments

Access & Security

  • Access Hours: Varies by location; most offer 24/7 access
  • Your Lock: You must provide your own lock for your unit
  • Gate Access: You'll receive a unique access code for entry
  • No Loitering: Only authorized tenants allowed on premises
  • Surveillance: Facilities may have security cameras for safety
  • Right to Inspect: Management reserves the right to inspect units if policy violations are suspected

Move-Out Procedures

  • Notice Required: Provide written notice by the 25th of the month
  • Clean Unit: Remove all items and leave unit clean
  • Lock Removal: Remove your lock before final move-out
  • Final Inspection: Management will inspect unit before deposit refund
  • Outstanding Balance: All rent and fees must be paid in full
  • Deposit Return: Refunded within 30 days if unit is left in good condition

Lien & Auction Policy

  • Late Payment: Failure to pay rent may result in a lien on your stored property
  • Notice: You will receive written notice of delinquent account
  • Auction: Unpaid units may be sold at public auction per Missouri state law
  • Stay Current: Keep your account current to avoid auction proceedings

General Rules

  • No living or sleeping in storage units
  • No running businesses from storage units
  • Drive slowly and carefully on premises
  • Keep units clean and pest-free
  • Respect other tenants' property and privacy
  • Report any suspicious activity to management
  • Children must be supervised at all times
  • No smoking on facility grounds
